About the Shop

Planet Argon Bicycles, founded in 1913, builds and sells unique, quality bicycles. Whether you need a fixie or a tall bike, a unicycle or a recumbent, PA Bikes will deliver the goods.

Sick of wasting your precious time searching for a parking spot or sitting in traffic? Biking is a great form of exercise, a great way to get to work, and a great method of reducing pollution and road congestion. Best of all, everyone looks sexy riding a bicycle.

Ready to take one of our gorgeous bikes for a spin? We'll help match you up with your perfect 2-wheeled companion.
